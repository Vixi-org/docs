Welcome to Vixi. Vixi helps you turn what you know into an interactive course, without spending weeks building it by hand.

You bring the expertise. Vixi's AI drafts the lessons, gives them a friendly on-screen avatar and voice, and turns the whole thing into a gamified course your learners move through lesson by lesson.

## What Vixi is

Vixi is a course creation tool for experts and creators. You describe a topic, answer a few questions, and Vixi generates a full course for you to review, refine, and publish.

Your finished course is not a static document or a video to sit through. It is an interactive experience: learners progress through lessons one at a time, talk with an AI avatar that teaches in your chosen voice and tone, and stay motivated by a built-in reward system of **hearts**, **pearls**, and **streaks**.

You work in the Vixi creator app at `https://create.vixiai.co`.

<Note>
The course creation wizard is designed for desktop. If you sign up on a phone, finish your first course on a laptop or desktop for the best experience.
</Note>

## Who it's for

Vixi is built for people who know their subject and want to teach it without becoming full-time course producers:

- **Subject-matter experts** turning hard-won knowledge into a structured course.
- **Creators and coaches** who want an interactive, engaging format instead of a slide deck or a long video.
- **Educators** who want to reach learners online and track how they're doing.

You do not need any technical or design background. If you can describe what you want to teach, you can build a course.

## What you can do

With Vixi you can:

- **Generate a course from a topic** using the AI wizard, then edit any lesson.
- **Ground the course in your own material** by adding knowledge sources such as a LinkedIn profile or a PDF.
- **Give your course a face and a voice** with a default or custom AI avatar.
- **Make learning feel like a game** with a themed learning map and a hearts, pearls, and streaks reward system.
- **Publish and share** your course with a link so learners can enroll.
- **See how learners are doing** and manage who can access each course.
