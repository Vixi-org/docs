Your account settings are where you keep your personal details current and tune a few preferences for how Vixi works for you. Everything here lives in one place, organized into clear cards so you can find what you need quickly.

## Opening settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sidebar">
    Sign in at `https://create.vixiai.co` and look at the navigation on the left.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Settings">
    Select **Settings** from the sidebar. You land on the **Account Settings** tab by default.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Settings area has two tabs at the top:

- **Account Settings** — your personal info and preferences (covered on this page).
- **Subscription** — your current plan and billing.

Switch between them with the tabs directly under the page heading.

## Account Settings tab

This tab is split into two cards: **Basic info** and **Preferences**.

### Basic info

The **Basic info** card holds the core details of your account:

- **First Name**
- **Last Name**
- **Email**

Edit any of these fields, then click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the page.

<Note>
  The **Save Changes** button stays disabled until your details are valid. Your first and last name each need at least a few characters, and your email has to be a properly formatted address. Once everything looks right, the button becomes active.
</Note>

### Preferences

The **Preferences** card covers notifications and the app's language.

#### Newsletter emails

Under **Send emails about product updates and announcement**, choose **Yes** or **No** to say whether you'd like to hear from Vixi about product updates and announcements.

#### Language

Vixi's interface is available in **English** and **Arabic**.

To switch, click the language button in the **Preferences** card:

- It reads **Switch to Arabic** when you're using English.
- It reads **Switch to English** when you're using Arabic.

<Tip>
  The language change applies immediately across the whole app — you don't need to click **Save Changes** for it to take effect.
</Tip>

## Subscription tab

The **Subscription** tab shows your current plan and gives you a way to manage billing. From there you can review your plan, upgrade, and open the billing portal to update your card or view receipts.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I change my password?">
    The Account Settings tab covers your name, email, newsletter preference, and language. It does not include a password field, so password changes aren't made here.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does changing the language affect my courses?">
    No. The language toggle changes the language of the Vixi interface you see as a creator. It applies right away and doesn't change the content or language of the courses you've built.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where did Earnings and Payouts go?">
    Earnings now live in their own **Earnings** section in the sidebar rather than inside Settings. Settings holds your account details and subscription.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
